Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are some common queries.

The average cost for a decent single-room hostel in prime locations like Boring Road or Kankarbagh ranges from ₹6,000 to ₹8,500 per month. This usually includes basic amenities like mess food (twice a day), Wi-Fi, and electricity. Shared rooms are more affordable, typically starting from ₹4,500.

Several institutes in Patna have excellent track records for IIT-JEE, including FIITJEE, Aakash Institute, and Allen Career Institute. The "best" one often depends on individual learning styles, faculty experience, and study materials. We recommend visiting the centers, attending a demo class if possible, and speaking to current students before making a decision.

Co-living spaces offer more flexibility, modern amenities (like gyms, recreation areas), and a stronger community focus, but they are often more expensive. Traditional hostels are more budget-friendly and cater specifically to student needs like fixed meal times. Your choice depends on your budget and lifestyle preferences.

Admission to most undergraduate courses at Patna University is based on the marks obtained in the Class 12th board examinations, followed by a counseling process. For some professional courses and postgraduate degrees, the university conducts its own entrance exams (PUCET). Always check the official Patna University website for the latest admission notices and procedures.
